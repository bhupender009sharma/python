-> BACKTRACKING - Backtracking is a general algorithm for finding
 all solutions to some computational problems, notably constraint
 satisfaction problems, that incrementally builds candidates to
 the solutions, and abandons a candidate as soon as it 
determines that the candidate cannot possibly be completed to a 
valid solution.
